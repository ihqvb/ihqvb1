About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival game where your only goal is to stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect power-ups, and upgrade your abilities to face the never-ending horde.

Inspired by popular rogue-lite games, Monster Survivors combines simple controls with deep strategic gameplay. Every run is different, with procedurally generated enemy waves and random power-ups making each playthrough a unique challenge.

With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty curve, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for casual players and hardcore gamers alike.

How to Play

Controls

  • Desktop: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character.
  • Mobile: Use the virtual joystick on the bottom left of the screen.
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range.
  • Use spacebar (desktop) or tap the button (mobile) to use special abilities.

Objective

Survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded by monsters.
  • Prioritize leveling up for more powerful abilities.
  • Collect gems dropped by defeated enemies to gain experience and level up.
  • Choose upgrades that complement your character's strengths.
  • Watch for special treasure chests containing rare and powerful items.
